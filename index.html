Psychology of Computer Interactions & Well-being
09:30 UK
Fri 10 Jul
⌄
About the session
Does the psychological impact of AI adoption strike a chord in your psyche?

Siobhan explores the psychology behind how we interact with technology, and what changes when AI becomes a constant presence in engineering workflows and people processes. As AI adoption scales across software development and team management, she examines how trust, over-reliance, and decision fatigue affect not just productivity but cognitive load and job satisfaction, and shares practical strategies for designing human-AI interactions that enhance well-being rather than erode it.

What you'll take away
  • 1How the way we interact with computers and AI shapes how we feel at work.
  • 2Practical ways to keep wellbeing front of mind as AI becomes part of daily work.
  • 3What "safe AI" looks like in practice, and why it matters for all of us.
Who it's for

Open to all colleagues. Recommended for users of AI.
